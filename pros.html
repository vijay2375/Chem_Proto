Advantages & Disadvantages

Advantages

  • 💰 Low cost – cheap and easy materials
    • 🎨 Custom colors – mix your own shades
  • 🌱 Eco-friendly – natural inks are safe
  • 🧪 Learning fun – understand chemistry

Disadvantages

  • ⚠️ Chemical risk – needs care & safety
  • ⏳ Time-taking – slower than ready ink
  • 🎭 Uneven quality – color may vary
  • 🧴 Short life – dries or spoils fast
